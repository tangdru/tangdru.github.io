This i3 is a data visualization based on the Cell Stem Cell 10th anniversary theme of lineage tracing. Using Scopus it illustrates both the evolution of the stem cell field and the way new research builds on work that came before. Users can navigate the graphic and the represented papers by stem cell type, organism, and author.
